How To Play

Welcome to Rock, Paper, Scissors!

  • When the game says "Go!" choose one of the elements in the game area and click on it.
  • Your choice will be highlighted with a O around it.
  • The computer will then pick one of the elements.
  • Their choice will be highlighted with a O around it.
  • The game will then display who won, and update the score slider at the bottom.
  • Your score is the section of the bar with the same colour as your choice is highlighted with.
  • The computer's score is the same colour as their choice is highlighted with.
  • You can adjust the difficulty of the game by choosing whether to have 3 or 5 elements included, by clicking the relevant button.
